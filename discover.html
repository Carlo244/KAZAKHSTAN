Discover Kazakhstan

A land of breathtaking landscapes, rich traditions, and a fascinating blend of East and West.

Geography of Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is the world's 9th largest country, covering 2.7 million square kilometers. It shares borders with Russia, China, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, and Turkmenistan. The landscape features mountains, deserts, lakes, and vast steppe lands.

Languages Spoken

Kazakhstan is a multilingual country, with Kazakh as the official language and Russian widely spoken.
